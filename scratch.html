How to Win a Point

  1. Serve Correctly
    • Use an underhand motion
    • Keep both feet behind the baseline
    • Serve diagonally into the opponent's service box
  2. Control the Rally
    • Keep the ball in play
    • Target your opponent’s weaker side
    • Use dinks, volleys, and smashes strategically
  3. Force an Error or Hit a Winner
    • Make your opponent miss or hit into the net
    • Hit a shot they can’t return
